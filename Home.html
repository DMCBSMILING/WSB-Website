About Us

Summary

The owner of this business is working in Tuna Manufacturing Company in Toril Fish Port Davao City. Then, They started doing business in just 2 kilos of Tuna meat. Until it becomes 10 kilos, then hundred kilos, until they already imported Tuna Products from other countries to serve the needs of the clients or buyers. At first the name of the business is WSB Seafood Distributor on March 1, 2014. And change it on January 2023 as One Davao Tuna Business. From Toril Davao City, there business is now reaching nationally (Luzon, Visayas, Mindanao).


Vision

One Davao Tuna Business aims to be globally competitive in seafood trading businesses.


Mission

To supply each household with healthy and delicious meal to serve each table and according to customers needs and satisfaction.
